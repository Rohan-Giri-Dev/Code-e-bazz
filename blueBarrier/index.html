Platform Modules

1. Dynamic Alert & Prediction System

This module serves as the analytical core of the platform.

  • Vulnerability Identification: Analyzes satellite data and historical flood records to identify regions susceptible to flooding and storm damage.
  • Predictive Analytics: Uses ML models to identify patterns and find coastal areas with similar topographical characteristics that may be vulnerable.
  • Population-Density Analysis: Integrates demographic data to prioritize resource deployment and evacuation efforts.

2. Interactive Predictive Mapping

Provides an intuitive, visual interface for data-driven decision-making.

  • Weather-Style Coastal Forecast: Interactive map of India's coastal regions that functions like a weather forecasting app for coastal threats.
  • Granular Data Access: Users can search for specific regions using pin codes to get localized threat assessments.
  • Enabling Targeted Alerts: Authorities can trigger hyper-local alerts through public announcement speakers, targeted SMS, and WhatsApp messages.

3. Collaborative Multi-User Interface

Streamlines emergency response with dedicated portals for different stakeholders.

  • NGO & Agency Portal: Verified organizations can log in and manage their profiles, listing available resources.
  • Centralized Resource Management: Provides a real-time overview of support capacity in any given area.
  • Efficient Coordination: Helps authorities efficiently coordinate with multiple NGOs based on threat level and capabilities.

Post-Hackathon Implementation Plan

To maximize the platform's impact, we recommend a phased implementation of physical infrastructure, guided by the data from BlueBarrier.

Construction of Coastal Wall Barriers

Using the platform's vulnerability mapping, the government can strategically construct seawalls and barriers in the most critically affected areas, similar to the protective infrastructure used in Japan.

Installation of Public Alert Systems

Deploying a network of powerful speakers in coastal communities and fishing villages to broadcast real-time voice alerts from the platform.

Deployment of an Integrated Sensor Network

Enhance the coastal barriers with a suite of sensors to feed live data into our platform:

  • Water Level & Pressure Sensors: To detect storm surges and tsunami waves.
  • Rain Gauges & Soil Moisture Sensors: To predict flooding and potential erosion or embankment collapse.
  • Ground Movement Sensors: To detect structural instability in the barriers.
  • Surveillance Cameras: To provide real-time visual confirmation of conditions.

Future Scope
